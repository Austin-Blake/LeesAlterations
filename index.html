Mrs. Lee, Who is she?

A mother, a wife, and a talented seamstress. She met her husband while he was serving in the US military, leaving her family behind to follow her heart and her husband back to Indiana.

She not only brought her loving, caring personality but also a artistic skill that has been, all but lost over the years in this country.

Quality, work ethic and love is sewn into this company.
